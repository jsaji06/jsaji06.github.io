Hey there, I'm Joshua Saji and I am a (to-be) second-year student studying Computer Sciences at the University of Wisconsin - Madison. I am originally based in Rockville, Maryland, which is just 30 minutes away from Washington, D.C. As someone who has picked up programming when he was just 10 years old, I have found a deep passion for innovation in the world of technology. Nine years down the line, I am well-versed in various areas of programming, including full-stack web development, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.

Outside of programming, I enjoy reading (currently Seneca's Letters to Lucilius), weightlifting, playing basketball, cooking, and writing. At my university, I am part of a magazine known as the Wisconsin Business Review, where I have one published article outlining my thoughts on the nature of generative art. I have a few article ideas I want to get around, so if you like what I've written so far, feel free to keep on the look out for a few new articles throughout this month.

Right now, I am creating a React Native application that combines my love for cooking with artificial intelligence, as somewhat outlined by the SmartChef project listed below. I've also made a web linear regression visualizer around a year ago, which is also listed on the projects header. Throughout the next few months, I will be focusing on creating more advanced, high-level applications and projects and list them here.
